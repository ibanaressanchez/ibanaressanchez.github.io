I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at the London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE), where I am affiliated with STICERD's Economics of Environment and Energy (EEE) Programme. I work on environmental and development questions, with a focus on urban and regional economics. I use tools from applied microeconomics, spatial economics, and empirical industrial organisation, combining structural models with primary data collection, field experiments, and historical sources.

I will be on the 2025-26 academic job market. You can find my CV here.
